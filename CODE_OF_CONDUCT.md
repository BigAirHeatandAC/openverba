# Code of Conduct

VoiceFlow adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1**, a widely used code of
conduct for open-source communities. The full, canonical text lives here:

> https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

Please read it — by participating in this project you agree to uphold it.

## Our pledge (summary)

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming, respectful,
and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity,
or experience level. We pledge to act in ways that keep the community open,
friendly, and constructive.

## Our standards (summary)

Behavior that helps the community:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its users
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Behavior that is not acceptable includes personal attacks, trolling, deliberate
intimidation, publishing others' private information, and any other conduct a
reasonable person would consider inappropriate in a professional setting. The
full Contributor Covenant (linked above) describes these in detail.
