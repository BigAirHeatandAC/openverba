Does my recording or data leave my PC?

Not in local mode. The screenshots, your narration and your typing are processed on your own machine â€” transcription runs locally with faster-whisper and the guide is written by a local vision model via Ollama. What do I need to run it?

Windows 10 or 11 and a microphone. For free local AI you install Ollama with a vision model (such as Qwen2.5-VL); a GPU makes it faster but isn't required. Alternatively, supply an Anthropic Claude API key and you don't need a local model at all.
